SESHADRIPURAM COLLEGE

Seshadripuram College is a prominent institution in Bangalore, established in 1973, with a focus on holistic student development and quality education. It is known for its strong academic programs, especially in commerce and management, and is affiliated with Bangalore City University. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including BBA, BCom, BCA, and MCA
